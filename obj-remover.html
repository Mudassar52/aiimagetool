FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

What types of objects can be removed?
Our AI can remove backgrounds from portraits, product photos, animals, cars, and more. It works best with clear subjects against distinguishable backgrounds.
What format is the output file?
The output is always a PNG file, which supports transparent backgrounds. If you apply a background color, that color will be embedded in the PNG as well.
Is my image uploaded to a server?
The image is temporarily sent to our AI processing API to remove the background. It is not stored permanently and is deleted immediately after processing. Your privacy is protected.
Can I change the background color after removal?
Yes! After the background is removed, you can click any of the color swatches or use the custom color picker to set any background color before downloading.
Is there a file size limit?
We recommend images under 10MB for the fastest results. Very large images may take slightly longer but are still supported.
