FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about Sticker Maker.

How do I use the sticker on WhatsApp?
Download the WebP sticker file, then use a WhatsApp sticker pack creator app (like "Sticker Maker" on Android/iOS) to import the sticker and add it to your personal sticker pack on WhatsApp.
What images work best for stickers?
Images with a clear subject against a simple or contrasting background work best — portraits, objects, animals, logos. Complex backgrounds or very detailed subjects may require manual touchup after background removal.
Can I turn off background removal?
Yes — toggle off "Remove Background" in the settings. The image will still be cropped and resized to 512×512 WebP, but the original background will be preserved.
Is the background removal free?
Background removal uses the remove.bg API which has a free tier. Disabling "Remove Background" converts images entirely in your browser with no API calls or limits.
