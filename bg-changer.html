FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers about the BG Changer tool.

Is BG Changer free to use?
Yes — BG Changer is completely free. Upload images, choose any background, and download your result in full HD with no watermarks and no account required.
What background types can I use?
You can choose from solid colors (with a full color picker and 20+ presets), beautiful gradients (including sky, sunset, ocean, and more), or upload your own custom background image in JPG, PNG, or WEBP.
How does the background blur work?
When you use a custom image background, a blur slider lets you apply 0–30px of blur to create a professional bokeh/depth-of-field effect — great for headshots and portraits.
Will the cutout quality be good for hair and fine details?
Our tool uses the remove.bg AI engine which handles complex edges including hair, fur, and semi-transparent objects with very high accuracy. Best results come from well-lit photos with clear subject separation.
Is my image stored or shared?
No. Your images are sent to the AI API solely for processing and are never stored on our servers. The final compositing happens entirely in your browser — your custom backgrounds never leave your device.
Can I change the background multiple times before downloading?
Yes! Once the AI has extracted your subject, you can switch between colors, gradients, and image backgrounds as many times as you like — all changes are instant and non-destructive.
