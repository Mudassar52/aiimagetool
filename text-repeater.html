FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions
How many times can I repeat text?
You can type any number into the "exact number" field — up to 9,999 repetitions. For very large outputs, we recommend downloading as a .txt file instead of copying.
Is my text saved anywhere?
No. Everything runs in your browser only. No data is sent to our servers. You can even use this tool offline once the page is loaded.
Can I use a custom separator between repetitions?
Yes! Choose from Newline, Space, Comma, or Pipe — or select "Custom" and type anything you want as the separator between each repetition.
Does it work with multi-line text?
Absolutely. You can paste paragraphs or multi-line content into the input. The entire block will be treated as one unit and repeated the specified number of times.
What does prefix and suffix do?
A prefix is added before every repetition, and a suffix is added after. For example, prefix "• " turns each repeat into a bullet point. Suffix "." adds a period at the end of each.
