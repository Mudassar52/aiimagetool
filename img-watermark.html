FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

Are my images uploaded to a server?
Never. All watermarking happens entirely inside your browser using the HTML5 Canvas API. Your images never leave your device, so they remain completely private and secure.
Can I use a transparent PNG as a logo watermark?
Yes — uploading a PNG with a transparent background as your logo is the recommended approach. The transparent areas will be preserved, so only your actual logo graphic appears on the image.
What does the Tiling option do?
Tiling repeats your watermark in a grid pattern across the entire image instead of placing it in a single position. This makes it much harder to crop out the watermark, offering stronger protection.
Can I watermark multiple images at once?
Yes — upload as many images as you like and click "Apply to All Images." The same watermark settings will be applied to every file. Download them all with the ZIP button.
What image formats are supported?
You can upload JPG, PNG, WEBP, BMP, and GIF images. The watermarked output is saved as a high-quality JPG by default. PNG inputs with transparency are preserved when exported.
