Privacy Policy

We take your privacy seriously. Learn exactly how AI Image Tools handles your data — and why we believe the best privacy is processing everything locally on your device.

Last Updated: June 1, 2025

100% Browser-Based

All image processing happens directly in your browser. Your files never leave your device.

No Data Collection

We do not collect, store, or share any personal information or image data.

No Account Required

Use all our tools completely free without signing up or providing any personal details.

1

Introduction

Welcome to AI Image Tools. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you use our suite of browser-based image tools, including our Background Remover, Background Changer, Image Converter, Image Compressor, and Image Resizer.

By using our tools, you agree to the practices described in this policy. If you have any questions, please contact us using the information provided at the bottom of this page.

2

How Your Images Are Processed

This is the most important section of our Privacy Policy. All image processing on AI Image Tools is performed entirely within your web browser. Here is exactly what that means:

  • When you upload an image, it is loaded into your browser's memory using the HTML5 File API — no data is transmitted to our servers.
  • All transformations — resizing, compression, format conversion, background removal — happen using browser-native APIs such as the Canvas API and WebAssembly.
  • Your images are never uploaded, stored, or transmitted to any external server at any point during processing.
  • When you close the tab or refresh the page, all image data is permanently cleared from memory.

In simple terms: your photos stay on your device. We have no technical ability to access, view, or store anything you process on our platform.

3

Information We Do Not Collect

We want to be fully transparent. AI Image Tools does not collect any of the following:

  • Your name, email address, or any other personal identification information.
  • The images, photos, or files you process using our tools.
  • Your IP address or precise geographic location.
  • Browser fingerprints or device identifiers.
  • Any account or login credentials (no account system exists).
  • Payment information (all tools are completely free).
4

Cookies & Analytics

We may use minimal, privacy-respecting analytics to understand how many people visit our site and which tools are most popular. This helps us improve our platform.

  • Analytics data is anonymized and aggregated — it cannot be used to identify any individual user.
  • We may use standard browser cookies for basic site functionality such as remembering your preferred settings.
  • We do not use advertising cookies, cross-site tracking cookies, or third-party marketing trackers.
  • You can disable cookies in your browser settings at any time without affecting the core functionality of our tools.
5

Third-Party Services

Our website may use the following third-party services. Each operates under their own privacy policy:

  • Google Fonts — Used to load typography (Sora and DM Sans). Google may log font requests from your browser. See Google's Privacy Policy for details.
  • CDN Providers — Some JavaScript libraries (such as JSZip) are loaded from public CDNs (e.g., Cloudflare). Standard request logs may be created by these providers.

We carefully select third-party services that are minimal and do not involve sharing your personal image data or any sensitive information.

6

Data Security

Because we do not collect or transmit your images or personal data, the risk associated with a data breach is fundamentally minimized. However, we still maintain good security practices:

  • Our website is served over HTTPS, ensuring all connections between your browser and our servers are encrypted.
  • We do not maintain any user databases that could be compromised.
  • Our tools are designed with a privacy-first architecture — local processing is not just a feature, it is the foundation.
7

Children's Privacy

AI Image Tools does not knowingly collect any information from children under the age of 13. Our services are general-purpose tools intended for users of all ages. Since we collect no personal information from any user, no special data risks exist for younger users.

If you are a parent or guardian and have concerns about your child's use of our tools, please contact us.

8

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time as our tools evolve or as regulations change. When we make changes, we will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.

We encourage you to review this policy periodically. Continued use of our tools after any changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

9

Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have certain rights regarding your data under laws such as GDPR (Europe) or CCPA (California). Because we do not collect personal data, most of these rights are satisfied by default. Nevertheless:

  • You have the right to know what data we collect — the answer is: none.
  • You have the right to deletion — since we store nothing, there is nothing to delete.
  • You have the right to opt out of any data sale — we do not sell data.
  • If you believe we have inadvertently collected any data about you, please contact us and we will investigate and respond promptly.

Questions About Our Privacy Policy?

If you have any questions, concerns, or requests regarding this Privacy Policy or how we handle data, we'd love to hear from you.
