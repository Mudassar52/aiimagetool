FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about BG Eraser. Can't find your answer? Feel free to reach out.

Is BG Eraser completely free to use?
Yes! BG Eraser is 100% free. You can remove backgrounds from as many images as you like without any signup, subscription, or hidden fees. No watermarks are added to your downloads.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPG, PNG, WEBP, and BMP formats. The maximum file size is 12MB per image. Your result is always delivered as a high-quality transparent PNG file.
How does the AI background removal work?
Our tool is powered by remove.bg's state-of-the-art AI model. It analyzes your image, identifies the main subject, and precisely removes the background — handling complex edges like hair, fur, and transparent objects.
Is my image data safe and private?
Absolutely. Your images are processed securely and are never stored on our servers or shared with third parties. Once your result is ready, the data is immediately discarded.
Can I process multiple images at once?
Currently, BG Eraser processes one image at a time. After downloading your result, click "New Image" to start with another photo. Bulk processing is coming soon!
What if the result doesn't look right?
Results are best when the subject is well-lit and clearly separated from the background. If you're unhappy with a result, try uploading a higher resolution or better-lit version of your photo. Our AI handles most images with 95%+ accuracy.
