FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How does AI upscaling work?
AI upscaling uses intelligent algorithms to analyze pixel patterns and predict how missing pixels should look when the image is enlarged. This produces much sharper, cleaner results compared to traditional resizing methods.
Are my images uploaded to a server?
All upscaling is processed directly in your browser. Your images never leave your device, so they're completely private and secure. No account required.
What image formats are supported?
The upscaler supports JPG/JPEG, PNG, and WEBP input formats. You can export the result as JPG, PNG, or WEBP depending on your needs.
What is the maximum upscale factor?
You can upscale images up to 4× their original resolution. A 500×500 image becomes 2000×2000 at 4× scale — perfect for print or large displays.
Is there a file size or count limit?
There's no enforced limit — process as many images as your browser can handle. Large images at 4× scale may require more memory, so very large batches may slow down on older devices.
