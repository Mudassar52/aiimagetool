FAQ
Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about DP Maker.

Is DP Maker completely free?
Yes! DP Maker is 100% free. Remove backgrounds, crop in circles, and download at any size without any signup, subscription, or hidden fees. No watermarks are ever added.
What image formats are supported?
We support JPG, PNG, WEBP, and BMP formats up to 12MB. Your final DP is always exported as a high-quality PNG file.
Can I use a transparent background?
Absolutely. Select the "Transparent" swatch (checkerboard pattern) to export a PNG with a fully transparent background — perfect for use on any platform or design.
Which size should I use for Instagram / WhatsApp?
For Instagram, use 1080×1080 px for the sharpest quality. For WhatsApp, 500×500 px is ideal. LinkedIn recommends 400×400 px. All sizes are available in one click in the Export Size panel.
How accurate is the AI background removal?
We use remove.bg's state-of-the-art AI which handles 95%+ of photos accurately, including complex hair, fur, and fine edges. For best results use a well-lit photo with the subject clearly separated from the background.
Is my photo data safe?
Your images are processed securely via remove.bg and are never stored on our servers. Once your result is ready, image data is immediately discarded.
